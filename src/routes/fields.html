HOW IT WORKS

STEP 01

We enable your field.

A QR sign goes on the fence and a facility account is created — sponsor boards, privacy policy, and settings flow down to every game automatically.

STEP 02

The crowd films.

A parent scans the QR, picks a side, and films. Every extra phone adds an angle; a fixed cam can anchor it if you want a guaranteed feed.

STEP 03

Every game streams.

Games broadcast free with a live scorebug and AI commentary; the venue and sponsors cover it. Teams keep the replays they want.
